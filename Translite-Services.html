Website Translation Services

Reach Out To The World With Our Website Localization Tool Translate your website into any language with a click!

  • Best Quality & Service

    Translite automatically crawl your website and translate the text to your desired language using either Google or Bing Translators based on your preference. You can then update the content to further improvise the quality of localized content. We have the experience and industry leading translation technology to ensure your website is translated quickly with the highest linguistic quality and technical accuracy, as well as being kept up-to-date with your source website changes and updates. Trusted by global companies from around the world, Translite provides quality and end-to-end website translation solutions that your business can depend for years to come.

    Speed and Simplicity

    Translite.Me website translation process is all about simplicity, speed and scalability. Like many companies before you, chances are you’ve invested time, money, and valuable resources in building your website. The last thing you want to do is go through another complicated, lengthy, and expensive process to translate your website into French, Chinese or any other language in which your firm plans to expand its business to. The good news is you don’t have to! Translite can now get your website translated as easy as 1-2-3 with speed and scalability. Our innovative website translation solutions are helping hundreds of businesses to reach global customers quickly and easily.

  • We Value Your Time

    Translite understand the importance of time. While you are browsing the website ,you wanted to quickly change the localized content of one sentence or word. You don’t need to go through the heavy process of logging into the backend portal and update the content. As a Owner of the website you can simple update the content with our inline editor. It’s that simple to update the localized content.

  • Dynamic Content

    We acknowledge your complexities; we provide seamless integration for your dynamic content. Modern website translation often requires translating a large number of small, incremental updates into many languages quickly and frequently. You can pass your original strings by calling our API. We will return the translated strings right away. We can handle both HTML and Plain Text effectively and efficiently. Still not enough, Download our SDK and integrate it with your web page for more complex localization requirements.

  • Cloud Ready

    Translite website translation solution is a hosted solution using Microsoft Azure Cloud. With this, our clients never have to worry about setting up physical servers in different parts of world to serve content to local customers and ensure optimal performance anywhere. Translite will automatically scale up geo location wise to deliver website content at fast speeds to your local customers around the world and around the clock.
